About

Welcome to my Homepage! I’m passionate about developing apps and games, and you’ll find a range of my projects showcased here. From practical applications to engaging games, I’m constantly experimenting with new ideas and refining my skills. I regularly update my blog, where I delve into the art of overthinking mundane topics from an analytical perspective—offering insights and unique viewpoints on everyday subjects. In addition to coding, I enjoy sketching and exploring new libraries and technologies in my spare time. If you’re interested in my work or have any questions, feel free to reach out. Thank you for visiting!
